Stroke Mortality Rate. Data

2 Datasets were combined in this study:

  • 1.Stroke Mortality Data Among US Adults (35+) by State 2015-2017, Dataset in U.S. Department of Health & Human Services.
  • 2.Median Household Income 2019, USDA Economic Research Service, U.S. Department of Agriculture


Stroke Mortality Rate. General

3 years averaged (2015-2017) Stroke Mortality Rate per 100,000 people mapped by each State, filters - gender: overall , ethnicity: overall.

  • Averaged USA mortality rate is around 75.
  • South East region is above average with Alabama leading at 100 death rate.


Stroke Mortality Rate. Median Household Income

Stroke mortality Rate vs Median Household Income.

  • Visible negative correlation between Stroke rate and median household income.
  • Clear correlation with median household income for South East region.


Stroke Mortality Rate. Race/Ethnicity

Race/Ethnicity: 3 years averaged (2015-2017) Stroke Mortality Rate per 100,000 people mapped by each State, filters - gender: overall , ethnicity: all.

  • Black Stroke rate is significantly higher than overall
  • Hispanic and Asian is lower than overall.


Stroke Mortality Rate. Gender

Gender: 3 years averaged (2015-2017) Stroke Mortality Rate per 100,000 people mapped by each State, filters - gender: all , ethnicity: overall.
