Stoke Exploration and analysis

Sourcing and Data Gathering

We sourced our data from the Stroke Prediction Dataset on the Kaggle website.
The dataset was downloaded as a .csv file to explore and clean for data analysis and modeling.

We chose to "Stoke Prediction Database" as it was rich with information regarding the incidence of stroke and permitted us to develop a robust model would attempt to predict it.

Initial Data Cleanup

“Other” gender category, considered non-impactful, was dropped from the dataset, resulting in removing 1 row of data.

In reviewing the raw data, the bmi attribute was identified as having 201 “N/A” values. This represents 3.9% of the dataset. The mean bmi value of 28.89 was used to impute the “N/A” values.

Gender, Marriage Status, and Residence were set up as Lables (1/0 for yes / no and rural / urban ).

One Hot - Data Cleanup

One Hot endcoding was use to split out Work_Type (5 types) and Smoking_Status (4 types) features.

There are 1,544 members of our dataset with "Unknown" as there smoking status. We researched medical literature in other studies where smoking status was "Unknown". We found the unknown was treated as at data category and that attempt to imput it tended to bias study outcomes. We chose to leave it as a data category (feature).

Flask Processing - Data Cleanup

We found fields with dashes and spaces that were causing our Flask processing to error out.

Three field names were updated:
Dash to underscore and lower case in "work_type_Self-employed"
Space to underscore in "smoking_status_formerly smoked"
Space to underscore in "smoking_status_never smoked"

Leading Causes of Stroke

Age increases your chances for stroke. The older you are, the more likely you are to have a stroke. Unfortunately Anyone can have a stroke at any age. Certain things can increase your chances of having a stroke.

Conditions

Common medical conditions that increase chances of having stroke are Previous stroke, High Blood Pressure, Heart disease, Diabetes.

Behavior

Common lifestyle choices that increase chances of having stroke are Obesity, too much Alcohol , Tobaco Use.
