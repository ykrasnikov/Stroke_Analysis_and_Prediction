Introduction

The objective of the Project is to develop a preliminary screening tool which can be used to identify the likelihood of an individual having a stroke based on general contributing factors.
Project consist of 3 main parts: stroke mortality rate analysis, leading causes of stroke analysis , data exploratory analysis and ML model building. Data [1] from Kaggle was used as the basis for the predictive model.

Stroke Facts

A stroke occurs when the blood supply to a region of the brain is suddenly blocked or when a rupture occurs starving the brain cells of oxygen and nutrients. Blockage obstructing the flow of blood to a region of the brain is called an ischemic stroke and accounts for 87% [3] of all strokes. The rupturing of blood vessel is called a hemorrhagic stroke and accounts for 13% [4] of all strokes.

Leading Causes of Stroke

Age increases your chances for stroke. The older you are, the more likely you are to have a stroke. Unfortunately Anyone can have a stroke at any age. Certain things can increase your chances of having a stroke.

Conditions

Common medical conditions that increase chances of having stroke are Previous stroke, High Blood Pressure, Heart disease, Diabetes.

Behavior

Common lifestyle choices that increase chances of having stroke are Obesity, too much Alcohol , Tobaco Use.
